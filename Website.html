The making process of the WakaHuia

Waka Huia – A traditional Māori treasure box.

Description:

This webpage is to showcase Henry Vincent's design from his product class. The gallery above shows multiple images of his creation the "Waka Huia", including the making process and use by the stake holder (Henry's Grandma). The Waka Huia is used to hold special adornments such as pendants and huia feathers for dressing the hair. They were created by the Maori orignally in the early 1800s.
